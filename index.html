Research

Learning-enabled robots with explicit physical structure.

01

Diffusion policy discovery

Mining rare but valid behaviors from diffusion policies, repairing them with sampling-based trajectory optimization, and folding them back into training.

02

Certified generative planning

Combining flow matching, control barrier functions, convex optimization, and prescribed-time dynamics for constraint-aware robot motion generation.

03

Multimodal VLA systems

Extending vision-language-action policies with physical sensing, thermal perception, and runtime safety filters for real-world manipulation.
